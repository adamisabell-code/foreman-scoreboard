The story

I have a day job. I have bills. I looked at my monthly expenses — $11,998 a month — and asked: what if an AI agent could replace all of it?

Not someday. In 90 days. Starting from zero.

So I built one. I named him Foreman. Every morning before I go to work, he files a report — what he researched, what he wrote, what he built, what the numbers say. I spend 20 minutes reviewing it. That's the whole system.

I'm documenting every step publicly: the TikToks, this newsletter, the live scoreboard. No edits. No cherry-picking. If it fails, you'll see it fail.

Week 1 is done. The numbers are real. They're not impressive yet. That's the point.
